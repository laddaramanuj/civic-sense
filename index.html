The Vision

Amrut Civic Connect bridges the gap between individual actions and governance rewards. By aggregating data from verified public systems, we create a transparent "Civic Score" that honors those who contribute daily to a better society.

Impact & Rewards

Real-world value for responsible citizens and governments.

Fast-Track Service

Priority processing for municipal licenses and permits based on your Civic Score.

Utility Discounts

Points-based rebates on electricity and water bills for high-compliance citizens.

Insurance Benefits

Lower health and vehicle insurance premiums for individuals with high safety ratings.

Strategic Roadmap

Phase 1: Pilot Launch

Initial rollout in selected Tier-1 cities with municipal data integration.

Phase 2: State Expansion

Unified state-level portal and RTO/Police record syncing.

Phase 3: National Platform

Integration with Digital India initiatives and banking ecosystems.
